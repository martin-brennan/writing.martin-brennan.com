Easton Porter is a thirteen-year-old boy, child of [[Fred Porter]] and [[Christie Porter]] the protagonist of both [[swamp_lullaby|Swamp Lullaby]] and [[louisiana_dreamin|Louisiana Dreamin']], who has psychic powers and is tormented by [[big_billy_claret|Big Billy Claret]] after narrowly escaping the swamp-man, but losing his father in the process. Easton also features as a secondary character in [[the_thaw|The Thaw]].
